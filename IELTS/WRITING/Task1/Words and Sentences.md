# Sentence

## 1.倒装句

介词前置的倒转

From identical vertical tubes nearby burst row upon row of lettuces.

1. 在桌子上放着一本书。→ On the table lies a book.
    
2. 在房间里没有人。→ In the room, there is no one.
    
3. 在黑暗中，我们能看见远处的灯光。→ In the darkness, we could see the lights in the distance.
    
4. 在那座山的顶端，你可以看到整个城市的美景。→ At the top of the mountain, you can see the beautiful view of the entire city.
    
5. 在角落里，有一只害羞的小猫躲着。→ In the corner hides a shy little cat.
    

助动词或者情态动词提前

Opposite, in narrow, horizontal trays **packed（过去分词修饰）** **not with** soil **but with** coconut fibre, **grow（谓语）** cherry tomatoes, shiny aubergines and brightly coloured chards.

## 2.**分词短语作状语**：

使用现在分词或过去分词开头的短语来作为句子的状语，增加句子的复杂度。

- 例子：Having completed the project ahead of schedule, the team was given a week off.
    

## 3.**虚拟语气（在条件句中）**：

用于表达与现在事实相反的假设情况。

- 例子：Were I in charge, I would implement more rigorous environmental policies.
    

## 4.**主语从句**：

当一个句子的主语是另一个句子时，称为主语从句。

- 例子：What we need most is a new strategy to increase our market share.
    

## 5.**宾语从句**：

作为动词或介词的宾语的从句。

- 例子：The committee decided that the proposal would be revisited in the next meeting.
    

## 6.**表语从句**：

紧跟在系动词之后，作为主语的补充说明。

- 例子：The main issue is whether the current policy is effective in reducing pollution.
    

## 7.**同位语从句**：

对名词进行解释或提供更多信息的从句。

- 例子：The fact that the global temperature is rising at an unprecedented rate is alarming.
    

## 8.**让步状语从句**：

使用although, even though等引导，表达让步关系。

- 例子：Although many people are aware of the environmental issues, few take actual steps to make a difference.
    

## 9.**原因状语从句**：

使用because, since, as等词引导，表达原因或理由。

- 例子：Since public transportation has become more efficient, more people are using it for their daily commute.
    

## 10.**目的状语从句**：

使用so that, in order that等词引导，表达目的。

- 例子：Governments should implement stricter regulations so that air pollution can be significantly reduced.
    

# Words

## 表示方便某人做某事

1. **Enable** - Giving someone the means or ability to do something.
    
    - "The online platform enables customers to purchase goods from home."
        
2. **Assist** - Offering help or support to make an action easier.
    
    - "The guidebook assists travelers in navigating the city."
        
3. **Aid** - Providing assistance or support.
    
    - "The tutorial videos aid users in mastering the software."
        
4. **Simplify** - Making something less complex or easier to do.
    
    - "The new procedure simplifies the application process for applicants."
        
5. **Support** - Offering assistance or enabling someone to do something more easily.
    
    - "The mentorship program supports young entrepreneurs in developing their business ideas."
        
6. **Facilitate** - Making an action or process easier or less difficult.
    
    - "The app facilitates communication between team members."
        
7. **Empower** - Giving someone the authority or means to do something.
    
    - "The training course empowers employees to manage their own projects."
